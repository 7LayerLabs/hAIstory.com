## The Real Constraints

Five things — not algorithms — will decide how fast this actually happens.

1. **Electricity.** Nuclear restarts, grid buildout. Frontier labs are signing 20-year power agreements. May be the real bottleneck.
2. **Chip supply.** NVIDIA, TSMC, geopolitics around Taiwan. A single fab outage could reshape the race.
3. **Data.** The "data wall" — we've used most of the high-quality public text on Earth. Synthetic data is the bet.
4. **Capital.** $100B+ training runs are coming. Infrastructure is trillion-dollar scale.
5. **Regulation.** EU AI Act, US executive orders, China policy. Frontier models may face export-control regimes like advanced semiconductors do.

## Where Power Moves Next

| Era | Power holder |
|-----|--------------|
| 1950–2005 | Universities |
| 2010–2018 | Big Tech |
| 2018–2022 | OpenAI + hyperscalers |
| 2023–2026 | GPU owners + frontier labs |

When compute is commoditized and models are shared, the moat moves to electricity and physical embodiment. Whoever can power and embody AGI first defines the next industrial era.
